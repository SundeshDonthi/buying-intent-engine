About

The Buying Intent Engine.

The Buying Intent Engine helps Marketing, Business Development, and Sales spend time on the accounts most likely to buy. Enter a company and it reads what is already public, weighs the buying signals, and points you to the accounts worth a conversation, with evidence you can check.

Built as an MVP

To prove the concept quickly, this first version uses only:

  • Publicly available data sources
  • Free and public APIs
  • Agent-powered analysis

Because of those choices it will not catch everything yet. Treat the score as a strong starting point that your judgment confirms, not a final verdict.

What it tracks

The engine checks 14 live signals across positive, negative, and context categories. Open the Signals tab for the full list, the source behind each one, and the ideas we are exploring next.
